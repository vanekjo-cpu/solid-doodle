## Employee Work Device Monitoring Agreement

**Company:** [Company Name]
**Effective Date:** [Date]
**Device Type:** Android Work Phone/Tablet

### I. Purpose

The Company provides work devices to employees to perform their jobs effectively and securely. To protect Company data, intellectual property, and security, the Company monitors these devices for security threats and policy violations.

### II. What Is Monitored

The Company monitors:

✅ **Monitored Items:**
- Network connections (WiFi, cellular data)
- USB device connections
- Installed applications (list only, not content)
- File access to Company folders
- Security events (failed login attempts, etc.)
- Device status and health

❌ **NOT Monitored:**
- Personal files or personal cloud storage
- Personal emails or personal messaging apps
- Employee location or GPS
- Keyboard logging or screen recording
- Microphone or camera access
- Social media accounts
- Personal browsing history

### III. Monitoring Methods

Monitoring is performed through:
- Automated security agent software installed on the device
- Analysis of network traffic to Company systems
- Regular security scans
- Alerts for suspicious activities

### IV. Data Retention

Monitoring data is retained for **90 days** and then automatically deleted, unless a security incident requires longer retention for investigation.

### V. Data Protection

All monitoring data is:
- Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Accessible only to authorized security personnel
- Protected by access controls and audit logging
- Subject to strict confidentiality agreements

### VI. Your Rights

As an employee, you have the right to:

1. **Access:** Request to see what data has been collected about your device
2. **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
3. **Deletion:** Request deletion of data after the retention period
4. **Explanation:** Ask for explanation of any findings or alerts
5. **Dispute:** Challenge findings if you believe they're inaccurate
6. **Complaints:** File a complaint with HR if you believe monitoring is inappropriate

### VII. How to Request Your Data

To request access to your monitoring data:
1. Email security@[company].com with subject "Data Access Request"
2. Include: Your name, device ID, and date range
3. Response within 10 business days

### VIII. What Happens If Issues Are Found

If monitoring detects potential security issues:

1. **Investigation:** Security team investigates
2. **Notification:** You'll be notified if it affects you
3. **Resolution:** We'll work with you to resolve it
4. **No Punishment:** Security findings aren't used for disciplinary action unless policy violation is confirmed
5. **Documentation:** Findings documented in your employee file (if serious)

### IX. Non-Retaliation

The Company will not retaliate against employees for:
- Requesting access to their monitoring data
- Exercising their rights under this agreement
- Reporting security concerns
- Filing complaints about monitoring practices

### X. Personal Use

Work devices are for business use only. Limited personal use is permitted, but:
- Personal use should be minimal
- Personal activities may be monitored under the same policy
- Private passwords should not be used for personal accounts
- Personal data should not be stored on work devices

### XI. Device Loss or Theft

If your device is lost or stolen:
1. **Immediately notify** security@[company].com
2. Device will be remotely wiped if possible
3. Your monitoring data is deleted
4. Investigation may be conducted

### XII. Termination

Upon employment termination:
- All monitoring ceases immediately
- Device data is wiped (if device is returned)
- Your monitoring records are archived per legal requirements
- Device is retrieved within [X] days

### XIII. Policy Changes

The Company may modify this monitoring policy with 30 days' notice to employees. Continued use of the device constitutes acceptance of changes.

### XIV. Questions or Concerns

For questions about this policy:
- Email: security@[company].com
- HR Contact: [name/email]
- Anonymous Hotline: [number]
